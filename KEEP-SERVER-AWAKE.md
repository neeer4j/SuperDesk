# Keep Render Server Awake (100% FREE)

## Problem:
Render free tier sleeps after 15 minutes of inactivity, causing 30-60 second delays.

## Solution: UptimeRobot (FREE Forever)

### Setup Instructions:

1. **Go to UptimeRobot:**
   - Visit: https://uptimerobot.com/
   - Click "Free Sign Up"
   - Create free account (no credit card needed)

2. **Add Monitor:**
   - Click "+ Add New Monitor"
   - **Monitor Type:** HTTP(s)
   - **Friendly Name:** SuperDesk Server
   - **URL:** `https://superdesk-7m7f.onrender.com`
   - **Monitoring Interval:** 5 minutes (free tier allows this)
   - Click "Create Monitor"

3. **That's it!**
   - UptimeRobot will ping your server every 5 minutes
   - Server will NEVER sleep
   - Completely FREE forever
   - No credit card required
